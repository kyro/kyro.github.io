About

I'm a product builder designing experiences that restore agency.

Trained as a physician, then moved into building. Co-founded Zipdrug, where we reimagined pharmacy access around the patient - delivery, adherence, and care coordination meeting people where they actually were. The company was acquired by Elevance Health. Now at Everlywell, I lead product for consumer and diagnostics, designing systems that put health insight directly in people's hands at the moment it can inform action. Different problems, same principle: capability belongs at the point of use.

Interested in AI, deliberately low-fidelity tech, wearables, biotech, psychology, and psychedelic-assisted therapy. I write about embodiment and design.
